About Aleia

A heavy bookworm in youth, Aleia cultivated a love for reading and learning and grew to become passionate about math and science. Wanting to be an Inventor, she studied Robotics at University and is working towards another in Data Science. In her free time, she works to become an Entrepreneur and focuses on programmining, robotic design, and space technology as to build a medium to combine her, and others, passions for literature, tech, and community.

Aleia spends everyday thinking on her purpose and what it takes to achieve it. Putting in time to learn as much as possible about Science and Technology, Finance, Business, Writing, and Personal Relations so she can apply all she's learned to give back and show others how to find that same place within themselves.
